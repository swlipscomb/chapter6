My Favorite Hobby: Backpacking

Backpacking is by far my favorite hobby. Backpacking is an activity where you camping overnight in nature, carrying all of your gear on your back. Backpacking for everyone looks different, but when I backpack, I carry about 30 pounds of gear in a special backpack, hike 7-10 miles to a location, and sleep overnight in a small tent.

You can backpack in any natural place that allows for backpackers, but the best places are in national parks. My favorite place to backpack is in Yosemite National Park. I love seeing the glacial domes, forests, rivers, and waterfalls.
